About Us

MASS Bonsai is an ecommerce website for bonsai plants. Its main aim is to provide seasonal, beautiful and healthiest bonsai plants to the people who love trees at a reasonable price.Bonsai plant is a unique piece of Asian aesthetic culture. This website is for people who are eager to train trees to grow short as per their wishes using traditional as well as modern techniques. One of the bestfeatures of our website is that it mostly provides bonsai plants that are useful and easy to maintain at a suitable price rate and offers free shipping all over Nepal. Our company, MASS Bonsai, has become a brand with multiple branches in a short period of time.
